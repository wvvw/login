AllThis first hit the radar in October 2011 thanks to a post on mashable. They had a particularly troubling feature: people who had never even heard of allthis found that allthis had profile pages for them, inviting people to buy 10 minutes of their time. This reached a head in December 2011 with a widely-circulated blog post criticizing the choice. AllThis proceeded to dial back the aggressiveness of this feature over the following months, but finally announced their closure in July 2012

The allthis.com domain is now parked.
